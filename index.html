About Mladen Žigmundić

Mladen Žigmundić is a web developer with bachelor's degree in physics. Although physics has a special place in his heart, at university his attention was especially grabbed by the programming subjects, because in order to conduct an experiment all that he needed was a computer.

After graduating he decided to steer his career direction towards web development. His biggest challenge so far has been finding out what it is that he likes more: front end or back end. After experimenting with both ends he decided to stick with front end technologies which he studied during the last year.
